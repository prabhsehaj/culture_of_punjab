The Heartbeat of Punjab

Punjab is known for its lively culture and vibrant dance traditions, which reflect the spirit of joy, celebration, and togetherness. Each dance form tells a story and is deeply rooted in the cultural heritage of the region.Punjab's folk dances, including Bhangra, Giddha, Jhumar, Luddi, and Sammi, are integral to its cultural heritage. These dances not only serve as entertainment but also play a significant role in community bonding and cultural expression in Punjab.
